Lost Story

A simple story about a group of friends trying out a brand new rollercoaster starts to go terribly wrong. Best enjoyed with a small group of your mates, Lost Story is a very highly-rated story game that you should probably check out.

Can you get both of the different endings in the game? Will you manage to escape the theme park, or deal with King?

Arcade Night

Arcade Night is a relatively new story game and is updated regularly with new choices and narrative options when it hits a certain number of likes.

It's simple: you and a friend visit an Arcade a night, but things don't really go to plan. There are lots of different ways to play, and it's best enjoyed if you can get some friends together to join in.

Hotel Stories

Hotel Stories is a narrative about visiting a rather spooky hotel. You and some friends can wander through the corridors and hotel rooms, making choices that will impact the narrative.
