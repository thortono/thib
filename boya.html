Airplane 4

One of the most popular story games in Roblox right now is Airplane 4. While most of the game is about flying airplanes around different locations, there is a narrative that strings it all together.

Airplane 4 is regularly updated, has a fun story that is best enjoyed with a few friends and is clearly one of the most popular on the platform with close to 2,000 players at the time of writing.

2020 The Story

This story focuses on the crazy year of 2020 and it starts you off on a camping trip. There is both a good and bad ending to this game and it will take you a few playthroughs to experience them both.

Cruise Story

Cruise Story takes you on the vacation of a lifetime, but it seemingly takes a turn for the worst. There are four endings which include good, bad, evil, and secret outcomes.
